How it works

Connect the warehouse. Keep the definitions.

Point Kelvin at your warehouse

Use read-only credentials and keep compute, data residency, and permissions in the platform you already trust.

Promote dbt models into metrics

Kelvin reads certified models and exposes business-ready metrics with lineage back to source tables.

Share governed dashboards

Teams explore live numbers with query-level cost visibility and no per-seat penalty for wider adoption.

kelvin.yml
warehouse:
  type: snowflake
  role: analytics_reader
  database: production

metrics:
  source: dbt
  project: analytics_core
  models:
    - fct_revenue
    - fct_margin
    - fct_customer_health

billing:
  mode: per_query
  cost_controls:
    monthly_budget: 2500
    alert_threshold: 0.8
