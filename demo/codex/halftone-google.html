Studio / manifesto

Small by design. Slow on purpose.

Halftone is six people working between Tokyo and Berlin. We are not built for churn, trend uptake, or the comforting theatre of infinite options.

We prefer durable systems, printed proof, considered silence, and the sort of art direction that still looks awake after the opening week has passed.
