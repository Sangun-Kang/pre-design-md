§ 02 — Studio
We don't do trends. We do work that's still readable in ten years.

Halftone was started in 2019 by two designers who met at a print fair and disagreed productively for the next four years. The studio is intentionally small — six people, two cities, one shared archive of references that grows by about a thousand items a year.

We take on roughly twelve projects annually. We write our own copy. We answer our own emails. If you need a deck within twenty-four hours, we are not the right studio for you, and we will tell you so kindly.

— K. Mori & L. Schäfer, founding partners
