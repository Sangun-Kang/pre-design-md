The studio

We don't do trends. We do work that's still readable in ten years.

Halftone was started in 2019 by two designers who met at a print fair and disagreed productively for the next four years. The studio is intentionally small — six people, two cities, one shared archive.

We take on roughly twelve projects a year. We write our own copy. We answer our own emails. If you want a deck within 24 hours, we are not the right studio.
