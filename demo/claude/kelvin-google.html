How it works

From warehouse to dashboard in fifteen minutes.

Connect once, define metrics in dbt, share dashboards. No cube, no semantic layer to learn, no proprietary modeling language.

  • 1Connect your warehouse — read-only credentials, no data leaves your environment.
  • 2Point Kelvin at your dbt project. Your existing models become your metrics.
  • 3Build dashboards by referencing metrics, not by writing SQL twice.
  • 4Share — viewers don't need warehouse access. Permissions follow your roles.
# kelvin.yml — three lines to start
warehouse: snowflake
dbt_project: "./analytics"
workspace: "finance"

# That's it. Run `kelvin sync`.
