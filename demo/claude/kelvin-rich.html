§ 02 — How it works

From warehouse to dashboard in fifteen minutes.

Connect once, define metrics in dbt, share dashboards. No cube, no semantic layer to learn, no proprietary modeling language to bring up at the next architecture review.

  • i.Connect your warehouse. Read-only credentials. No data leaves your environment.
  • ii.Point Kelvin at your dbt project. Your existing models become your metrics, automatically.
  • iii.Build dashboards by referencing metrics, not by writing SQL twice.
  • iv.Share. Viewers don't need warehouse access. Permissions follow your roles.

1# Three lines to start. Run `kelvin sync`.
